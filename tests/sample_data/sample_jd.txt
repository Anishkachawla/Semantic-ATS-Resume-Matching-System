Senior Backend Engineer

We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our platform team.
You'll design and build the services that power our core product, working
closely with data and frontend teams.

Responsibilities:
- Design, build, and maintain scalable backend services and APIs
- Own data pipelines feeding our analytics warehouse
- Work in a containerized, cloud-native environment (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Participate in an agile development process with regular code review
- Mentor junior engineers on the team

Requirements:
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and experience with frameworks like FastAPI or Django
- Experience with SQL databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- Hands-on experience with AWS or another major cloud provider
- Familiarity with CI/CD practices and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience
- Strong communication skills and experience working in agile teams
